Privacy Policy

KanaHost is a managed AI assistant hosting platform operated by ContextX Inc, a Delaware company. This page explains what data we handle, what we do not do, and how we keep your AI assistant private on your own dedicated infrastructure.

✓

Bottom line up front

The fast version, in plain English.

  • Your assistant runs on a dedicated server for your account, not on a shared customer machine.
  • We do not train AI models on your data, and we do not routinely read your conversations.
  • Your infrastructure is hosted primarily at Hetzner in Germany, with traffic protected by Tailscale and Cloudflare.
  • If you choose to use outside AI models, requests may go to Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google so your assistant can answer you.
  • We only use essential cookies for sign-in through Clerk. We do not run ad trackers.
  • You can ask for a copy of your data, ask us to delete it, and use GDPR rights where they apply.

Section 1

What we collect

＋

KanaHost collects only the information reasonably needed to create, secure, operate, and support your account.

This may include:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, billing contact details, and account identifiers.
  • Service configuration information, such as the integrations, model providers, domains, or channels you choose to connect.
  • Operational metadata needed to run the service, such as server health, uptime events, crash logs, deployment events, API usage counts, and authentication events.
  • Support information you send us when you contact support or ask us to investigate a problem.

If your assistant processes emails, calendar entries, documents, tasks, or conversations, that content is handled on your dedicated environment so the service can work for you. We treat that content as your data.

Section 2

What we do not collect

＋

We built KanaHost around the idea that your assistant should belong to you, not become training fuel for someone else.

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not use your conversations, documents, or memory to train KanaHost models.
  • We do not routinely read or monitor your assistant conversations.
  • We do not run advertising trackers across the service.

There are only narrow exceptions: if you explicitly ask us for troubleshooting help and authorize access, if a system-generated error snippet appears in logs, or if we are legally required to respond to a valid legal process.

Section 3

How we use information

＋

We use information to provide the service you asked for, including to provision servers, secure accounts, operate networking, process payments, answer support requests, and improve reliability.

More specifically, we may use information to:

  • Create and manage your KanaHost account.
  • Deploy and maintain your dedicated AI assistant infrastructure.
  • Authenticate users and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.
  • Send service notices, billing notices, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Monitor uptime, investigate incidents, and keep the platform stable.

We do not use your private assistant content for targeted advertising or generalized model training.

Section 4

Hosting, storage, and security

＋

Customer infrastructure is primarily hosted on Hetzner servers located in Germany. We selected this environment to support strong operational control and European hosting options.

Your assistant runs on a dedicated server environment assigned to your account. Data is intended to stay within that environment except when it must be transmitted to a service you choose to connect, such as an AI provider, email provider, calendar provider, or identity provider.

We use multiple layers of protection, including:

  • Encrypted network access using Tailscale for private connectivity.
  • Cloudflare services for edge protection, DNS, and traffic management where applicable.
  • Encryption in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational controls to protect accounts and infrastructure.

No system can be promised as perfectly secure, but we design KanaHost to minimize exposure and keep customer data isolated by default.

Section 5

Third parties and subprocessors

＋

We use a limited set of third-party providers to operate KanaHost. Depending on your setup, your data may be processed by:

  • Hetzner for core server infrastructure in Germany.
  • Tailscale for encrypted private networking.
  • Cloudflare for DNS, edge security, and traffic routing.
  • Clerk for authentication and session management.
  • Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google when you or your configuration choose those model providers for AI responses or related processing.

These providers act as independent service providers or subprocessors. Their handling of data is governed by their own terms and privacy commitments. We aim to share only the data required for them to perform the requested service.

Section 6

Cookies

＋

KanaHost uses essential authentication cookies through Clerk so you can sign in securely and keep your session active.

We do not use ad-tech cookies for behavioral advertising. We do not use cross-site marketing trackers as part of the core service.

If we later add optional analytics or preference cookies, we will update this policy and, where required, ask for the appropriate consent.

Section 7

GDPR and your rights

＋

If the GDPR or similar privacy laws apply to you, you may have rights that include access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection, portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

We generally process account information to perform our contract with you, comply with legal obligations, protect the service, and pursue legitimate interests such as security, fraud prevention, and reliability.

To exercise privacy rights, email support@kana.host. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request.

Where KanaHost acts as a processor for customer content, the customer remains responsible for the lawfulness of the content they place into the system and for handling end-user requests where required.

Section 8

Retention, export, and deletion

＋

We keep account and billing records for as long as reasonably needed to operate the service, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Customer content generally remains in your dedicated environment until you delete it, export it, or your service ends, subject to limited backup and legal-retention needs.

You may request:

  • A copy of data we maintain about your account.
  • Deletion of your account and associated hosted environment, subject to legal or security retention requirements.
  • Reasonable assistance exporting customer data where feasible.

Deletion requests can be sent to support@kana.host.

Section 9

Changes and contact

＋

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect product, operational, legal, or regulatory changes. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last updated" date.

If a change is material, we may also provide notice through the service or by email.

Questions, requests, or privacy concerns can be sent to support@kana.host.
